Services

How can I help you?

Appraisals

I can appraise your collection for insurance or tax needs. My lengthy tenure as a coin dealer and my knowledge of the current market can be an invaluable resource. I am also an authorized submission center for NGC coin grading service. Service fees are based on the extent of the collection and the appraisal needs and can be performed at your location by appointment. My knowledge and list of prominent Coin Dealers nationwide will allow for immediate liquidation at the best market prices.

Estates

Estate liquidations are my specialty. I welcome all estate inquires, including requests from heirs and estate attorneys. After viewing your collection, flat rate appraisals can be provided for an immediate decision for liquidation. Upon request, I can provide necessary references for your review before my visit.

We buy coins

I will purchase any Gold or Silver Coin Collection (not steel pennies or foreign coins) at the best prices in the market.

You've worked hard to build a collection that you are proud of so when it is time to sell only trust it with a professional numismatist who is as thorough and attentive as you are. I travel from coast to coast to provide appraisals, estate services, and all your coin buying and selling needs.

Large collections can be liquidated by my cash offer or I can sell your coins by consignment. I am a credentialed member of several of the nation's largest Numismatic Associations. I will travel to buy your collection if the value warrants. Call or email us with your list today. You can also send your material via registered mail, insured for full value. Call me first, then ship the coins with a complete inventory. When I buy your coins, you can expect immediate payment with the return of your shipping expenses. If I am unable to buy your collection, I will return your coins promptly and refund your shipping expenses. Be sure to call in advance and send me a list of your inventory before shipment, so I know what to expect in advance.

Call or Email today to discover your coins value!
